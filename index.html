Clinical Focus

Where Innovation Meets Clinical Need

Our specialists work across key respiratory diseases where technology can create meaningful impact on patient outcomes.

COPD

Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease affects millions worldwide with progressive respiratory decline.

Innovation Opportunities

  • Early exacerbation detection systems
  • Remote monitoring platforms
  • Personalized rehabilitation tools
  • Smart inhaler technology

Asthma

Inflammatory airway disease requiring ongoing management and acute intervention.

Innovation Opportunities

  • Adherence tracking systems
  • Environmental trigger detection
  • Personalized treatment algorithms
  • Integrated care platforms

Interstitial Lung Disease

Progressive scarring of lung tissue requiring early detection and monitoring.

Innovation Opportunities

  • Early imaging detection AI
  • Disease progression monitoring
  • Symptom tracking tools
  • Tele-rehabilitation systems

Sleep & Ventilation

Respiratory conditions affecting sleep quality and ventilation efficiency.

Innovation Opportunities

  • Non-invasive monitoring devices
  • Sleep apnea detection tools
  • Ventilation optimization systems
  • Home-based diagnostic devices

Digital Health

Platforms for remote monitoring, virtual care, and patient engagement that extend care beyond the clinic.

  • Virtual pulmonary rehabilitation
  • Mobile asthma management
  • Tele-pulmonary consultations

  • Wearable respiratory sensors
  • Portable diagnostic tools
  • Advanced ventilation systems

  • Clinical decision support
